Frequently Asked Questions

What is My Glowfit?

My Glowfit is an all-in-one wellness application that uses AI to create personalized nutrition and workout plans, adapted to your specific goals and lifestyle.

How does AI personalization work?

Our algorithms analyze your age, weight, goals, and dietary preferences to generate unique meal plans and training routines that adapt as you progress.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you don't love the results, just send us an email and we'll refund the full amount.

Is there a free trial period?

We don't offer a free trial, but our 30-day money-back guarantee allows you to try the application risk-free.

How long until I see results?

Most users start feeling more energetic in the first week and see visible physical changes within 3-4 weeks of following their plan.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

My Glowfit supports Vegetarian, Vegan, Keto, Paleo, Gluten-Free, and Dairy-Free diets. You can configure this in your profile.

Can I use My Glowfit on my phone?

Yes! Our platform is fully optimized for mobile devices (iOS and Android) via web browser, so you can take it anywhere.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use bank-level encryption to protect your personal information. Your data is never sold to third parties.
